# MoodMirror Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 8, 2026

## Introduction

MoodMirror ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the MoodMirror mobile application (the "App").

## Data Collection and Storage

### What We Collect

MoodMirror collects the following data to provide emotion tracking services:

1. **Facial Images**: Photos captured during check-ins for facial expression analysis
2. **Voice Recordings**: Audio recordings for voice tone and sentiment analysis
3. **Text Input**: Notes and written reflections you provide
4. **Health Data** (Optional): Sleep and exercise data from Apple HealthKit if you grant permission
5. **Emotion Data**: AI-analyzed emotion scores, insights, and historical patterns

### How We Store Your Data

**ALL DATA STAYS ON YOUR DEVICE**

- All emotion check-ins, photos, voice recordings, and analysis results are stored locally on your device using Core Data
- We do NOT store your photos or voice recordings on any server
- We do NOT upload your personal data to our servers
- Your data is protected by iOS device encryption

### Third-Party Services

**Google Gemini AI**

- When you complete a check-in, we send a text description of your facial expression and voice tone to Google Gemini AI for emotion analysis
- We DO NOT send photos or audio recordings to Google
- Only anonymized descriptions are sent for AI processing
- Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Apple HealthKit** (Optional)

- If you grant permission, we access sleep and exercise data to correlate with your mood
- This data remains on your device and is only used to generate insights
- You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings

## Data Usage

We use your data solely to:

1. Analyze your emotions and provide personalized insights
2. Generate charts and visualizations of your emotional patterns
3. Correlate health data (sleep, exercise) with your mood if you opt in
4. Provide daily check-in reminders if you enable notifications

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT share, sell, or transmit your personal data to third parties.**

- Your photos and voice recordings never leave your device
- Only anonymized text descriptions are sent to Google Gemini for AI processing
- We do not have access to your personal data

## Data Control

### Your Rights

- **Access**: View all your check-ins in the Timeline
- **Export**: Export all your data to JSON format via Settings
- **Delete**: Delete individual check-ins or all data via Settings
- **Portability**: Exported data is yours to keep or transfer

### Data Retention

- Data is retained on your device until you delete it
- Deleting the app removes all local data
- You can delete all data at any time in Settings

## Permissions

MoodMirror requests the following iOS permissions:

1. **Camera**: To capture selfies for facial expression analysis
2. **Microphone**: To record voice notes for tone analysis
3. **Speech Recognition**: To transcribe voice notes
4. **Notifications**: To send daily check-in reminders (optional)
5. **HealthKit**: To access sleep and exercise data (optional)

You can manage these permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

## Children's Privacy

MoodMirror is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has used the app, please contact us.

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- iOS device encryption for local storage
- Secure communication with Google Gemini API (HTTPS)
- No server-side storage of personal data
- Regular security updates

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by:

- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Displaying a notice in the app
- Requiring acceptance of the new policy

## Legal

### California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:
- Know what personal data we collect
- Access their personal data
- Delete their personal data
- Opt-out of data sharing (we don't share data)

### European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EU residents have the right to:
- Access their personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Erase their data (right to be forgotten)
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control to exercise these rights directly within the app.
